Cookie Policy

Effective date: TBD (publish date) Last updated: 2026-05-03

This Cookie Policy explains how Main Street IQ LLC ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on mainstreetiq.com. It supplements our Privacy Policy.

If you have questions, email privacy@mainstreetiq.com.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They let the site remember your actions and preferences, measure how the site is used, and integrate features from third-party services. "Similar technologies" includes pixels, tags, web storage, and SDKs that work the same way for our purposes.

2. Cookies we use

We group cookies into three categories.

Strictly necessary

These run the site. Without them, basic features will not work. We do not need consent for these because they are essential to deliver what you asked for (loading the page, holding form state, security).

Cookie / technology Set by Purpose Lifetime
Session and security cookies Main Street IQ Page load, form integrity, basic protections Session
Cloudflare cookies (__cf_bm, cf_clearance) Cloudflare Bot detection, security Session to 30 days

Analytics

These help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it. We do not use analytics cookies for advertising. Where required by law (EU, UK, California for opt-out), we ask for or honor a choice before loading them.

Cookie / technology Set by Purpose Lifetime
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics 4 Aggregate usage metrics, page views, sessions. IPs are anonymized; no cross-site advertising linkage. Up to 2 years

Functional / third-party embeds

We embed a few external tools so you can interact with us. These tools may set their own cookies when you actively use them.

Tool When it loads What it does
Calendly When you open a meeting-booking widget or click a Calendly link Lets you book a meeting on Scott's calendar
Zoho Forms When you submit the contact form or Strategic Brief intake Sends form data to our CRM

What we do NOT use

  • No advertising cookies
  • No retargeting pixels (Meta, LinkedIn, X, TikTok, Google Ads)
  • No cross-site behavioral tracking
  • No session-replay tools (Hotjar, FullStory)
  • No A/B testing platforms that fingerprint visitors

3. Your choices

Your browser

You can refuse or delete cookies through your browser settings. If you block strictly necessary cookies, parts of the site may not work.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor the Global Privacy Control signal. If your browser sends GPC, we treat it as an opt-out request for any non-essential cookies and any sale or sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not sell or share for advertising regardless.

Opt out of Google Analytics

Install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on or block analytics cookies through our cookie banner if displayed.

"Do Not Track"

Browser-issued Do Not Track signals are not currently a uniform standard. We rely on GPC instead, which carries the same intent in a verifiable form.

4. Changes

If we add, remove, or materially change a cookie or tracking technology, we will update this page and bump the "Last updated" date. For material changes affecting active clients, we will email notice at least 30 days before the change takes effect.
