How the Engagement Works

Three Phases, Clean Handoff

01

Stabilize — Weeks 1 to 2

Full-time or near-full-time

Close the current month. Answer open questions from the board, auditors, and investors. Meet the team, audit the systems, and document what's running and what's broken. End of week 2: you have a clear picture of the function's state and a concrete stabilization plan.

02

Operate — Months 1 to 6

10–30 hours per week, scaled to the seat

Run the function. Monthly close, board reporting, FP&A, investor relations, cash management, team leadership. Improve what's obviously broken. Build the systems, documentation, and team structure that make the next permanent hire succeed from day one.

03

Hand Off — Months 6 to 9

Tapered, supporting the permanent hire

Define the permanent role scope, support the executive search, interview finalists, and onboard the new leader. Taper out once they're running the function confidently. The best engagements end with a stronger function than the one we stepped into.
