What to Expect

30 Minutes. No Pitch.

The call is structured but informal. Here's the rough shape:

  • First 5 minutes: Your context — company stage, what's working, what's broken in the finance function.
  • Next 15 minutes: The specifics — close cycle, margin visibility, cash forecasting, tech stack, what your current provider is doing (and not doing).
  • Last 10 minutes: Honest assessment — whether you need a fractional CFO at all, whether Mainstreet IQ is the right fit, and what I'd prioritize in the first 90 days if we worked together.

You'll leave with a recommendation, not a sales pitch. If we're not the right fit, I'll say so — and probably point you toward who is.
