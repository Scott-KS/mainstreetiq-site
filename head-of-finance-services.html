Who This Is For

When You Need a Finance Leader but Not a Finance Department

The title varies. The scope is the same — a single accountable owner for the whole finance function.

You're a Founder and the Books Are on You

You're running product, sales, and hiring. You're also the one pulling numbers for the board, chasing receivables, and deciding whether the burn is sustainable. You can't be the Head of Finance forever.

You're Post-Seed and the Investors Want Better Reporting

Your lead investor is asking for monthly dashboards, cohort data, and a real forecast. The bookkeeper isn't equipped for it. You're not ready to hire a full-time CFO. This is the gap we fill.

You're Mid-Market and Finance Has Always Been Outsourced

Your CPA does the books, a controller does the close, a planner does the forecast — and nobody owns the function as a whole. You need one person accountable for the whole thing.

You're Pre-Series A or Pre-Exit

You're six months from a raise or a sale. You need a credible finance leader in the data room and on investor calls. Bringing in a Head of Finance now — fractional or interim — beats showing up to diligence with a bookkeeper.

You Inherited a Finance Mess

You're a new CEO, a new PE sponsor, or a new operator. The previous regime left finance in rough shape. You need someone to come in, assess the damage, stabilize the function, and build the path forward.

You're a Solo or Small-Team Operator

You don't have a CFO, Controller, and Finance Director. You have you, maybe a bookkeeper, and a spreadsheet. A Head of Finance role consolidates everything into a single seat that actually makes decisions.

What The Seat Covers

End-to-End Finance Ownership

One seat, one owner, whole function. The operational and strategic work combined until the company is big enough to split them.

Close & Statements

Monthly close, GAAP statements, management reporting. Bookkeeper oversight or direct ownership at the smallest stage.

Planning & Forecasting

Annual budget. 13-week cash flow. Scenario planning. Runway analysis. The forward-looking work founders can't do alone.

Investor & Board Relations

Board decks, investor updates, due diligence responses, fundraise prep. The polish that gets deals done.

Systems & Infrastructure

Accounting stack, planning tools, reporting dashboards. Building the foundation so the function scales without adding headcount every year.

Engagement Models

Fractional, Interim, or Fast-Start

01

Fractional Head of Finance

10–20 hours per week, ongoing

Permanent finance leadership at part-time hours. The single owner of finance for companies that aren't ready for a full-time leader but need someone accountable. Most startup and lower mid-market companies start here.

02

Interim Head of Finance

Full-time, 3–6 months

You need someone running finance full-time right now — a leader just left, you're entering a raise, you're in diligence. We embed full-time, cover the seat, and hand off to a permanent hire when the moment calls for it. More on interim engagements.

03

Fast-Start Build

6–12 week project

For companies standing up a real finance function for the first time. We build the accounting stack, the reporting package, the forecasting model, and the month-end process — then hand off to your bookkeeper or incoming finance hire with documentation.
