      FAQ

      Common Questions

      What is the CCIQ Assessment?

      A free 6-minute, 14-question assessment for Central Coast wineries. You answer questions about your scale, channel mix, current marketing, top challenges, and growth ambition. The assessment produces a personalized CCIQ Profile naming your three highest-leverage outcomes for the next 12 months and a recommended starting point across the CCIQ catalog.

      Who is this assessment for?

      Owner-operator wineries on California's Central Coast (Santa Barbara, San Luis Obispo, Monterey counties and adjacent). Most useful for wineries between $300K and $10M in revenue who want to grow their wine club, lift DTC, capture AI visibility, or reduce wasted marketing spend.

      How long does it take?

      About six minutes. Fourteen questions, mostly multi-choice, with one revenue-mix grid where you estimate the percentage of revenue from each channel. No prep required. Your CCIQ Profile renders on screen the moment you finish.

      What does the CCIQ Profile include?

      Three sections: Where You Stand (your revenue band, production scale, tasting room model, wine club status, revenue mix observation), Your Highest-Leverage Outcomes (three outcome bullets with revenue impact bands scaled to your size), and Your Recommended Starting Point (the right CCIQ tier, with reasoning, pricing, and expected outcome).

      Is this a sales pitch?

      No. Your CCIQ Profile is yours regardless of whether you ever engage further. The recommendation may be Discover (free first issue), a one-time Diagnose project, a Monitor subscription, or a retainer. Scott reaches out only for the highest-priority profiles within 48 business hours, and only if you asked for a call.

      What happens to my data?

      Your responses and contact information are sent to our CRM (Zoho) so we can build and send your CCIQ Profile, plus the inaugural issue of the Wine Country Intelligence Report. We do not sell or share your data. Unsubscribe at any time.
