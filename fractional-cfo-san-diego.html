Why San Diego

The Finance Challenges Built Into This Market

San Diego's industry mix creates finance complexity that generic advisors don't understand. These are the specific pressures we see most often.

Biotech & Life Sciences Burn

R&D capitalization. Grant accounting. Milestone-based revenue from pharma partnerships. Equity-heavy comp. Life sciences companies in Torrey Pines and Sorrento Valley have financial profiles nobody else uses — and the wrong CFO hire costs you a year of runway.

Defense & Government Contracts

DCAA compliance. FAR cost principles. Indirect rate structures. Contract mix analysis. Naval Base San Diego and the surrounding defense ecosystem demand finance teams that can pass an audit and still forecast margin accurately.

Craft Beverage & DTC Brand Economics

San Diego's craft beer scene, kombucha makers, and fast-growing DTC beverage brands face the same challenge: thin margins, heavy COGS, distributor economics that shift without warning, and inventory tied up for months.

Cross-Border Supply Chain

Companies with operations spanning San Diego and Tijuana deal with transfer pricing, customs timing, dual-currency reporting, and working capital swings that a domestic-only CFO has never seen.

Our Framework

The RAID Operating System

Every engagement — biotech startup, defense primes subcontractor, DTC brand, or hospitality group — is built on the same four principles.

R

Redundant

Documented, cross-trained processes. No single points of failure. Your finance function runs whether any one person is there or not.

A

Agile

Automation-first. We build systems before we hire people. Technology handles the repetitive work so your team handles the thinking.

I

Intelligent

Your finance team operates as owner-operators, not scorekeepers. They don't just report what happened — they drive what happens next.

D

Data-Driven

Every team member owns 3–5 KPIs tied to real business levers. Real-time visibility into cash, margins, and the metrics that matter.
