Choose the Right Level of Support

Fractional CFO — Ongoing

Embedded finance leadership covering month-end close, cash and margin visibility, investor reporting, and cross-functional strategy without full-time costs.

Cadence: Weekly Best for: $15M–$100M companies

Monthly close management, cash flow forecasting, margin analysis, investor and board reporting, KPI dashboards, cross-functional strategy, and finance team development.

~10 hours/month

Oversight and strategic guidance for companies with competent finance teams seeking senior perspective on major decisions and growth planning.

Strategic review sessions, board prep support, financial model validation, growth planning, key decision advisory, and finance team mentorship.

Financial modeling, finance team assessments, close process redesign, M&A preparation, investor reporting packages.

Scoped deliverables with clear timelines: three-statement models, close process optimization, due diligence prep, reporting package builds, and finance function assessments.
