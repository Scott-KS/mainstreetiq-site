Building Finance Functions That Drive the Business Forward

Most fractional CFOs hand you a report and leave. I embed inside your company and build the finance infrastructure that lets you actually run the business in real time.

I specialize in building finance functions that operationalize business decisions -- not just track them after the fact. My career spans DTC, ecommerce, SaaS, and PE-backed companies ranging from $10M to $300M in revenue. I've seen what works and what breaks at every stage of growth.

My sweet spot is companies in the $15M to $50M range that have outgrown their current finance setup. The close takes too long, the data is unreliable, and the CEO is making decisions without real numbers. I fix that.

Before building Mainstreet IQ, I spent 12 years at Northern Trust in institutional finance -- modeling, M&A due diligence, and ERP implementation. That institutional rigor is what I bring to growth-stage companies that need it most.

I'm a Navy veteran with an MBA from Pepperdine's Graziadio School of Business. I bring discipline, structure, and operational intensity to every engagement.
